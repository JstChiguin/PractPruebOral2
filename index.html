Conversación: Practica las preguntas y respuestas tal como saldrán en la prueba oral.
🎾 2 ARRIBA - SPORTS
Yo
Have you played any sports recently?
🔊 Jav iu pleid eni sports rísentli?
Vicky
Yes, I have.
🔊 Ies, ai jav.
Yo
What did you play?
🔊 Uat did iu plei?
Vicky
I played soccer with my friends last weekend.
🔊 Ai pleid sóker uiz mai frends last uikénd.
Yo
Who did you play it with?
🔊 Ju did iu plei it uiz?
Vicky
I played it with my friends.
🔊 Ai pleid it uiz mai frends.
📱 2 ABAJO - LOSE YOUR PHONE
Yo
Have you ever lost your phone?
🔊 Jav iu éver lost ior foun?
Vicky

🔊 Ies, ai jav.

🔊 Uer did iu luz it?
Vicky

Yo

🔊 Uen did iu fáind it?
Vicky

🔊 Ai fáund it de séim dei.
